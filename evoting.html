Overview.

Voteme is voting app that helps to musianas to build up career smoothly. Peoples can vote their desired musicians through the app.

  • The problem
  • The goal
  • Music artists are required to online appliacation to publish their music albums and getting more votesa and appreciates, comments from thier flowers
  • Design fully responsive website to full fill the user’s needs. Design clear navigation, clear checkout process are required.

My Role

UX Designer

Project Status

Complete

Project Duration

Four Weeks

User Research.

To better understand the target user and their needs, I conducted user interviews, which I subsequently turned into empathy maps. I found that many target users use mobile application to rather than the website. Users have requested simple and clear interface to find and to do the voting.

Low-fidelity prototype.

To prepare for usability testing, I created a low-fidelity prototype that connected the user flow of voting for the desired musicians profile.

Usability study: findings .

Next, I sketched out paper wireframes for home page screen in my website, keeping the user pain points about navigation, browsing, and checkout flow in mind.

Mockups.

Based on the insights from the usability studies, I added a button as anonymous voting to go the voting page directly.

High-fidelity prototype.

The high-fidelity prototype followed the same user flow as the low-fidelity prototype, including design changes made after the usability study.
